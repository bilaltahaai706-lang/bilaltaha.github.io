01 / PROJECT
DMLS Component Redesign – Tool Life Optimization via Additive Manufacturing

Redesigned machine component parts at Atkore International using Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS) technology. The redesign consolidated two separate assemblies into a single monolithic part, leveraging the geometric freedom of additive manufacturing. Fillets were strategically incorporated at high-stress regions to eliminate stress concentration fracture points. The result was a 3× improvement in tool service life — from 2 months to 6 months — alongside a 30% reduction in tooling cost, while maintaining all mechanical performance requirements and reducing both material usage and post-processing time.

SolidWorks ANSYS DMLS DFMA FEA
